About the Team

The Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team is a multi-time World Champion team based in Brackley, UK. Known for engineering excellence, they have dominated F1 with drivers like Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg.

  • Founded: 2010 (modern era)
  • Headquarters: Brackley, UK
  • Constructors' Championships: 8 (2014-2021)

Star Driver: Lewis Hamilton

Sir Lewis Hamilton, a seven-time World Champion, is considered one of the greatest drivers in Formula One history. His partnership with Mercedes has rewritten the record books.

  1. Born: January 7, 1985
  2. Nationality: British
  3. Career Wins: 103+
  4. Podiums: 195+
